How Our Mortgage Calculator Works

Our free mortgage calculator helps you estimate your monthly payment by considering:

  • Home Price: The purchase price of the property
  • Down Payment: Typically 10-20% of home price
  • Interest Rate: Current market rates (varies by lender)
  • Loan Term: 15 or 30 years most common
  • Property Tax: Annual tax divided by 12
  • Home Insurance: Required by lenders
  • PMI: Private mortgage insurance if down payment < 20%

Why Use Our Calculator?

Complete Picture: Unlike basic calculators, we include taxes, insurance, and PMI to show your true monthly cost.

Amortization Schedule: See how much goes to interest vs principal each year.

Private: No signup required. Your data stays on your device.

Accurate: Uses standard mortgage formulas for reliable estimates.
